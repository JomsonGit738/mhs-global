About Courses

Our degrees that include a foundation year have been carefully created so that students acquire the abilities necessary to succeed at the degree level.

MHS provides the option of a four-year degree with foundation year for students who do not fulfil our usual entrance requirements for admission to one of our undergraduate degree courses.

Modules aid in the development of writing, maths, computer, public speaking, and presentation abilities as well as an awareness of the contemporary corporate environment. Prospective students should prepare themselves for substantial amounts of reading and independent study.

Candidates who wish to change careers or who want to go back to school may find this course very appealing. Our cutting-edge programmes guarantee that what you learn can be implemented realistically in the business world and that they will also equip you to handle the academic demands of a full degree programme.
